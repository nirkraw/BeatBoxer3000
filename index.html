Welcome to the BeatBoxer3000

The BeatBoxer3000 is virtual drum machine that allows you to record individual tracks, put them together in a beat, and hear it played back.

Would you like to take a tour?

Note* Use headphones for best results.

Press the corresponding letter on your keyboard or click the pad to hear the sound
Click the red button to start recording your sound for the corresponding pad.
You will hear 4 beeps to count off your recording.
Press the button again to end recording.
Once you have your sounds setup, click this big button to start recording. Press again to stop.
Click Play to hear it back!
Change the volume for any sound with the volume fader
